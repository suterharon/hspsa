Nurturing Kenyan Youth Through Arts & Mental Health

Holistic curriculums for empowered communities. We combine artistic expression with evidence-based mental health practices in local schools.

Explore Our Curriculum
The Framework

Mind, Heart, & Body

MINDful Expression

Focuses on creative thinking, problem-solving, and cognitive exercises to help students process complex thoughts.

HEARTfelt Connection

Builds emotional intelligence, resilience, and empathy through collaborative performance and visual arts.

Body of Work

Centers on physical wellness through movement-based art, large-scale mural painting, and kinetic stress-relief.

Partnerships & Events

We don't just provide a syllabus; we bring it to life. By partnering with local Kenyan schools, we host interactive events designed to destigmatize mental health discussions.

  • ✓ School-wide collaborative mural projects.

  • ✓ Teacher training workshops on art therapy basics.
